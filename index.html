About

I am an undergraduate student in Computer Science (CS) at the University of Waterloo. Before joining CS, I majored in Recreation and Leisure Studies.

My research lies at the intersection of Human-Centred Computing (HCI), Artificial Intelligence (AI), and CS Education. Currently, I am interested in exploring how users interact with AI systems and how iterative design can better align their expectations with outcomes. I am also a Karate enthusiast, bike rider, and traveller in my spare time.

You are welcome to email me if you have questions about undergraduate application, internal transfer to CS, or co-op job applications. I have gotten so much love and care through my life journey, and this is my way of giving back.
